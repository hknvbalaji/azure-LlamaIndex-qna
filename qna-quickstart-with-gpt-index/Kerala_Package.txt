ENCHANTING KERALA
Duration: 04 Nights / 05 Days
Destinations: Munnar • Alleppey • Kovalam
Traveller Type: Solo Explorer

------------------------------------
TOUR HIGHLIGHTS
------------------------------------
- Ideal solo-friendly itinerary at your own pace
- Scenic tea gardens & misty hill stations in Munnar
- Sunrise canoe ride on backwaters of Alleppey
- Overnight houseboat experience on Vembanad Lake
- Kovalam's famous Lighthouse Beach & Ayurvedic spa
- Explore Vizhinjam fishing harbour & local seafood
- Optional Kathakali cultural show
- All key sightseeing & transfers included
- Services of a professional driver throughout
- Safe, well-connected solo travel route

------------------------------------
DAY 1: ARRIVAL IN MUNNAR
------------------------------------
- Arrive at Cochin International Airport / Ernakulam
- Road transfer to Munnar (approx. 3.5 hrs) — scenic ghats en route
- Check in at boutique homestay & freshen up
- Evening stroll through Munnar town bazaar & local tea shops
- Dinner on own — try local Kerala sadya
Overnight: Munnar

**Note:**
- Carry light woollens — evenings in Munnar are cool (15–18°C in season)

------------------------------------
DAY 2: MUNNAR SIGHTSEEING
------------------------------------
- Breakfast at homestay
- Visit Eravikulam National Park — home of the Nilgiri Tahr
- Mattupetty Dam & Echo Point — serene lake & boating
- Tea Museum at KDHP — learn the art of tea-making
- Top Station viewpoint — panoramic views of Tamil Nadu plains
- Return to homestay; evening at leisure
Overnight: Munnar

**Note:**
- Eravikulam may be closed during Neelakurinji blooming season (every 12 years). Confirm locally.

------------------------------------
DAY 3: MUNNAR → ALLEPPEY (HOUSEBOAT CHECK-IN)
------------------------------------
- Early breakfast & check out
- Drive to Alleppey (approx. 3.5 hrs)
- Check in to houseboat by noon on Vembanad Lake
- Cruise through narrow canals, paddy fields & coconut groves
- Onboard lunch & dinner included with houseboat stay
- Sunset on the backwaters — one of Kerala's iconic solo experiences
Overnight: Houseboat — Alleppey

**Note:**
- Solo-friendly shared houseboats available for budget travellers
- Carry insect repellent for evenings on the water

------------------------------------
DAY 4: ALLEPPEY → KOVALAM
------------------------------------
- Early morning canoe ride through village canals (optional, highly recommended)
- Breakfast on houseboat & check out
- Drive to Kovalam (approx. 3 hrs)
- Check in at beachside resort / guesthouse
- Afternoon: Relax at Lighthouse Beach — swimming & sunbathing
- Explore Hawah Beach & Samudra Beach
- Evening: Ayurvedic massage session at a local spa (optional)
Overnight: Kovalam

**Note:**
- Always swim within lifeguard-flagged zones; rip currents can be strong off-season

------------------------------------
DAY 5: KOVALAM → DEPARTURE
------------------------------------
- Breakfast at resort
- Visit Vizhinjam fishing harbour & Vizhinjam Mosque
- Last stroll along the beach & souvenir shopping
- Transfer to Trivandrum International Airport for return flight
Tour ends with unforgettable memories of God's Own Country!

------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES FOR SOLO TRAVELLERS
------------------------------------
- Carry a valid photo ID — required for houseboat check-in & hotel registration
- Kerala is generally very safe for solo travellers; stay aware of rip currents at beaches
- Inform your homestay/resort of your daily plan for added safety
- Best connectivity: Airtel/BSNL in Munnar; Jio in Alleppey & Kovalam
- Carry cash — many backwater villages are not card-friendly
- Itinerary may be adjusted due to weather or local conditions; all inclusions will be honoured
